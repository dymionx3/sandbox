Frequently Asked Questions
What controllers are supported?

This tool supports DualShock 4, DualSense, and DualSense Edge controllers via USB connection.

Is this safe to use?

While this tool is designed to be safe, calibration involves modifying controller settings. Always backup your settings first and proceed carefully.

Will this void my warranty?

Modifying controller calibration may affect warranty coverage. Check your manufacturer's warranty terms before proceeding.

How do I reset to default settings?

The tool includes a reset function to restore factory calibration settings. This option will be available once your controller is connected.
